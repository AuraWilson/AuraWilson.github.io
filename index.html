Hi! My name is Aura Wilson and I am the founder of STRGIRLS. Born and raised in New York City.
STRGIRLS is a place where “Anyone can be a star!”. STRGIRLS is a non-profit organization to help females glide through life with some assistance!
Our mission is to help every female in need with life or guidance through tough times by finding a creative spark. I’ve decided to create this brand as a female that has been lost in many situations and needed guidance from another female like a big sister, this brand is to help those who also need a big sister. We accept ages from 13+.
